About Us

From the land of goodness to the world...... Arkatrade is a holding company that specializes in the manufacturing and freezing of fruits and other food products. The company has a strong focus on preserving the natural goodness and flavor of its products while ensuring that they are safe for consumption. Arkatrade uses modern technology and techniques to process and package its food products, and it maintains a high level of quality control to ensure that its products meet or exceed industry standards. The company's portfolio includes a wide range of fruits and other food products that are popular with consumers around the world. In addition to its manufacturing and freezing operations, Arkatrade also has a strong commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship. The company works closely with local communities and farmers to source its raw materials and implements eco-friendly practices throughout its operations. Overall, Arkatrade is a leading player in the food manufacturing and preservation industry, known for its commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation ......
